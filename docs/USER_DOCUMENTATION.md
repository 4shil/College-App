## Introduction

### About the App
The JPM College App is a comprehensive mobile application designed to digitize and streamline all college operations. It serves three main user types:

- **Students**: Access academic information, attendance, assignments, and campus services
- **Teachers**: Manage classes, mark attendance, create assignments, and submit reports
- **Administrators**: Oversee all college operations, manage users, and generate reports

### Supported Platforms
- iOS (iPhone/iPad)
- Android (Phones/Tablets)
- Web Browser

## Getting Started

### 1. Registration (Students Only)

#### Use Case
New students register for the app to access college services.

#### Steps
1. Open the app and tap **"Register"**
2. Enter your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email (must match college records)
   - Phone Number
   - Password (min 8 characters)
3. Select your:
   - Department (CSE, ECE, etc.)
   - Year of Admission
   - Program (B.Tech, M.Tech, etc.)
4. Tap **"Register"**
5. Enter the OTP sent to your email
6. Wait for admin approval (status: "Pending")

#### What Happens Next
- Admin reviews your registration
- You receive an email when approved
- Login with your credentials

---

### 2. Login

#### Use Case
All users login to access their dashboard.

#### Steps
1. Open the app
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. Tap **"Login"**
4. You'll be redirected to your role-specific dashboard

#### Troubleshooting
| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Forgot password | Tap "Forgot Password" ‚Üí Enter email ‚Üí Check inbox |
| Account blocked | Contact administrator |
| Invalid credentials | Verify email/password, check caps lock |
